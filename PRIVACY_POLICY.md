# Privacy Policy for PureMark

**Last Updated:** January 15, 2026

**Effective Date:** January 9, 2026

PureMark ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application ("App").

## 1. Information We Collect

### Information You Provide

**Dietary Preferences**
- Your dietary selection (Halal or Kosher)
- Your allergen selections (e.g., Peanuts, Tree Nuts, Milk, Eggs, Fish, Shellfish, Soy, Wheat, Sesame)

**Feedback**
- When you submit feedback, we collect: feedback category, your message, and any screenshots you choose to attach

### Information Collected Automatically

**Scan History**
- Product names (which you can edit)
- Ingredient lists extracted from images
- Analysis results and compliance status
- Scan timestamps
- Detected language of ingredient labels

### Information We Do NOT Collect

- Personal identification (name, email, phone number)
- Location data
- Device identifiers or advertising IDs
- Browsing history or behavioral tracking
- Financial or payment information
- Biometric data

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

- **Analyze ingredient lists** for halal/kosher compliance based on your dietary preferences
- **Identify allergens** in products based on your allergen selections
- **Maintain scan history** so you can review previous scans
- **Improve our service** through feedback you voluntarily submit

## 3. Data Storage

### Local Device Storage

The following data is stored **only on your device** using secure local storage:

- Dietary preferences
- Allergen selections
- Scan history (up to 50 most recent scans)

This data never leaves your device except during active scans (see "Image Processing" below).

### Server Storage

- **Feedback submissions** are stored on our servers to help us improve the App
- **Your latest dietary profile** may be stored to personalize your experience

## 4. Image Processing

When you scan an ingredient label:

1. You take a photo or select an image from your photo library
2. The image is sent securely (via HTTPS) to our servers
3. The image is processed **in memory only** to extract and analyze ingredients
4. **Images are NOT permanently stored** on our servers
5. Only the extracted text and analysis results are returned to your device

## 5. Third-Party Services

### OpenAI API

We use OpenAI's GPT-4o-mini API to parse and analyze ingredient text. When processing your scans:

- Extracted text is sent to OpenAI for ingredient analysis
- OpenAI's privacy policy applies to this processing: https://openai.com/privacy
- No personal information is shared with OpenAI

### Google Gemini API (via OpenRouter)

We use Google's Gemini 2.0 Flash model via OpenRouter for optical character recognition (OCR) to extract text from ingredient label images. When processing your scans:

- Image data is sent to Google's Gemini API through OpenRouter for text extraction
- Google's privacy policy applies: https://policies.google.com/privacy
- OpenRouter's privacy policy applies: https://openrouter.ai/privacy
- Images are processed in real-time and not permanently stored
- No personal information is shared with these services

## 6. Permissions We Request

### Camera Access

Required to take photos of ingredient labels for scanning. You can deny this permission and use the photo library upload option instead.

### Photo Library Access

Required to select existing photos of ingredient labels and to attach screenshots to feedback. Access is requested only when needed.

## 7. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information:

- All data transmission uses HTTPS encryption
- Local data is stored using secure storage mechanisms
- Images are processed in memory and not permanently stored
- No user accounts or passwords to compromise

## 8. Data Retention

### On Your Device

- Scan history is limited to 50 entries
- You can delete individual scans at any time
- Preferences are stored until you change them
- Uninstalling the App removes all local data

### On Our Servers

- Feedback is retained to help improve the App
- No personal identifiers are associated with feedback

## 9. Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

- **Access your data**: View your scan history and preferences within the App
- **Delete your data**: Remove individual scans or clear all history
- **Modify your preferences**: Update dietary and allergen settings anytime
- **Deny permissions**: Use the App with limited functionality without granting camera/photo access
- **Uninstall**: Removing the App deletes all locally stored data

## 10. Children's Privacy

PureMark is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us.

## 11. International Users

Our servers may be located in different countries. By using the App, you consent to the transfer of information to countries that may have different data protection laws than your country of residence.

## 12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically.

## 13. Medical Disclaimer

PureMark is designed to assist with identifying potentially non-halal or non-kosher ingredients. However:

- Results are for informational purposes only
- We do not guarantee 100% accuracy
- Always verify with religious authorities for definitive rulings
- Allergen detection is not a substitute for medical advice
- Consult healthcare professionals for serious allergies
